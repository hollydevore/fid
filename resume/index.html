HOLLY DEVORE

{an innovative creative dedicated to intentionally crafted designs}

//bio

Holly is a Graphic Design Student at Seminole State College, specializing in long document layout, prepress, and illustration. She describes her design style as “Simple Eclectic” or “Cozy Minimalist” and believes that a strong foundation in typography and colour are key to sending the clearest and most direct message to the viewer. "Intentionally crafted designs," she says, "speak a vital message before a word of the content is ever read." This is the power of a graphic designer, and one of the many reasons Holly is passionate about creating.

To see more of Holly’s work check out her Instagram and Facebook pages @hollydevoredesigns!
